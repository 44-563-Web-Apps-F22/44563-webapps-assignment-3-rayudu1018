description of Mascot

Auburn is a city in Cayuga County, New York, United States, at the north end of Owasco Lake, one of the Finger Lakes, in Central New York.

Three fun facts about the mascot:

  1. The region around Auburn had been Haudenosaunee territory for centuries before European contact and historical records.
  2. The alpaca is a species of South American camelid mammal.
  3. It is similar to, and often confused with, the llama.
